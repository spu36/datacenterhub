About Us

www.datacenterhub.org was established at Purdue University in 2014 to preserve data and digital infrastructure that was available after the Network for Earthquake Engineering Simulation (NEES) was terminated by National Science Foundation NSF

This site is made possible by University of Canterbury, New Zealand, and serves as historical repository of collections of files once housed at datacenterhub without the interactive tools that were available on that site
